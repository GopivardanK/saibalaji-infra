    SAI BALAJI INFRA (INDIA) PRIVATE LIMITED (An ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company)
    A BRIEF NOTE ABOUT OUR COMPANY
    I take the privilege of briefing you about the profile of my business entity which I had started in the year 1995, as a small concern and dealt with only small scale contracts in constructions. It was such a small passage of my starting business career through which I had efficiently and progressively stepped into the partnership business just after 6 years from 1995. From the year 2001 to 2006 I was deeply involved in back to back contract works at various places through partnership ventures in other companies.
    By the grace of God, in the Year 2006, I could start my own firm in the name of M/s.SAI BALAJI TECHNOLOGIES under my proprietor ship. Under this firm I undertook various contracts related to electrification and got applauded by many prestigious clients in this line.
    In the Year 2009 I had started a partnership firm in the name of M/s.SAI BALAJI INFRASTRUCTURES, having my wife Smt. Swapna as my partner. Under this partnership, I started various Civil Contracts also,, which were highly praised as efficient works by our reputed clients. There were a number of esteemed projects I undertook under my proprietor ship as well as my partnership.
    It was in the Year 2011, I launched a Private Limited Company in the name of M/s.”SAI BALAJI INFRA (INDIA) PVT LTD” (Though initially I wished to float private limited company with SAI BALAJI TECHNOLOGIES name, I could not do this since the non availability of this name at ROC). Now our company is in compliance with all statutory requirements like PF, ESI, GST, WORKS CONTRACT TAX and we have ISO 9001-2015 CERTIFIED COMPANY applied for ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001 and we are fulfilling various Legal and Constitutional requirements which a Private Limited Company ought to be followed. Our company has sufficient manpower to satisfy our reputed client’s requirements.
    Here with we are giving assurance to give a best Quality and workmen ship.
